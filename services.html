HOW IT WORKS

Our Seamless Process

1. Initial Consultation: We begin by understanding your specific goals and unique financial needs.

2. Document Collection: We guide you through gathering necessary files, including ITR sets, bank statements, and business certificates

3. Application Processing: Our experts handle the heavy lifting, leveraging our network of 50+ partners to fast track your application.

4. Approval & Disbursement: We manage the final hurdles to ensure your funds are successfully disbursed into your account

Application Checklist

1.Financial History: Full sets of your ITR for the last 2 years.

2.Banking Records: Bank statements covering the last 12 months.

3.Business Credentials: Certificate of Incorporation (COI) or Business Licenses (BL).

4.Audit & Tax Reports: Recent audit reports and GSTR-3B/GSTR-1 filings for the last year.

5.Obligation Sheet: A comprehensive list of your current loans and financial obligations.
